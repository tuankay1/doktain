About Our Company

DOKTAIN RESOURCES LTD (DRL) is an indigenous Marine, Engineering servicing company, registered to provide wide range of Building and Civil Works, Maintenance of mechanical and electrical component services, Corrosion control, Marine Construction and Procurement, Security services, Procurement of Marine and Industrial materials. We are positioned to render services to both public and private sectors of the Nigerian economy. DRL was incorporated as a private business in 2002 before operating for about ten years in the Oil and Gas sector with a business name- DOKTAIN RESOURCES LTD (DRL) with current share capital of 25 million.

DRL is the authorised distributors of RGEC products (pneumatic fenders and other marine spare parts) in Nigeria, Gabon, and Equatorial Guinea. Also, the distributors of all types of pipes for Eastern China Steels in Nigeria, Gabon, Equatorial Guinea, and Cameroon.
